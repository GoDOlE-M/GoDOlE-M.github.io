About Me

With over 5 years of experience as an Unreal Engine Game Developer and Programmer, I have honed my skills in crafting engaging and immersive gaming experiences. My expertise extends across advanced Blueprinting concepts, allowing me to design, prototype, implement, and test gameplay features and supporting systems. I have successfully packaged games for both PC and mobile platforms (Android).

My proficiency in multiplayer gameplay systems enables me to create dynamic and interactive environments, while my ability to develop sophisticated systems for NPC behavior and simulation ensures a lifelike and engaging player experience. I am adept at breaking down high-level tasks into manageable components, providing accurate estimates, and delivering polished results.

I possess strong mathematical, problem-solving, and analytic skills, which I apply to performance analysis and code optimization, ensuring smooth and efficient gameplay. My solid understanding of 3D graphics and physics systems allows me to collaborate effectively with animation teams to produce aesthetically pleasing NPC movements.

Technical Skills

  • Artificial Intelligence: Expertise in Behavior Trees, Blackboard, and Environment Query Systems.
  • Visual Effects: Proficient with Particle Systems, including Niagara and Niagara Liquid Systems.
  • Procedural Generation: Experience with Blueprint Building Generators for both exteriors and interiors.
  • Character Animation: Skilled in Advanced Locomotion Systems, Animation Blueprints, Blend Spaces, Animation Montages, IK Rig, IK Retargeting, and Level Sequencing.
  • Environment Creation: Proficient in building lighting, post-processing, materials, and textures.
  • Cloud Integration: Experience with Amazon Web Services, connecting PC and mobile games to databases using pSQL, API integration via VARest, and JavaScript.
  • Optimization: Expertise in Hierarchical Level of Detail (HLOD), profiling, and other optimization techniques.
  • UI/UX Design: Creating and implementing intuitive and user-friendly interfaces.

I am a highly motivated individual with a hands-on mentality, eager to contribute to an energetic and growing company. My goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of game development, delivering exceptional gaming experiences to players around the world.
